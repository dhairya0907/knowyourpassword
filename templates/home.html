Compromised Passwords

In a nutshell, compromised passwords are real passwords that have been exposed in data breaches. Because of this, they're no longer suitable for ongoing use, since they could be used to gain access to other account. You can search them online below or use APIs to integrate them into other online systems.

Know Your Password protects the privacy of searched passwords in same way as HIBP.
